# 💎 The AutoResearch Vision: "The Frontier Engine"

> **"Our goal is not to build models that solve problems; our goal is to build a system that discovers the science behind the solutions."**

---

## 🚀 The North Star: Autonomous Scientific Discovery
We are building the **Frontier Engine**—a domain-agnostic, self-evolving laboratory that automates the entire lifecycle of artificial intelligence research. We move beyond "AutoML" into **"Autonomous Science"**—where the system doesn't just tune hyperparameters, it discovers novel mathematical structures.

### 1. 🌍 Neural-Symbolic Intelligence (The Physics Engine)
The engine views every problem through the lens of **Mathematical Constraints**. 
- **Symbolic Verification**: We use `FakeTensor` and LSP-lite static analysis to "prove" code correctness before execution.
- **Meta-Feature Grounding**: Agents do not guess; they are fed exact dataset statistics (skewness, cardinality, sparsity) to anchor their reasoning in empirical reality.

### 2. 🧠 Institutional Memory & Cross-Pollination
The system's "Intelligence" compounds across missions.
- **Innovation Bridge**: Breakthroughs in NLP (e.g., attention variants) are autonomously adapted to Tabular or CV tasks via mathematical analogy.
- **Surrogate Intuition**: The system learns to predict failure (OOM, Divergence) based on past experiment logs, killing "unviable" hypotheses in milliseconds.

### 3. 🛡️ Adversarial Scientific Rigor
We enforce a **"Trust but Verify"** protocol using adversarial agents.
- **Skeptic Red-Teaming**: Every architecture must pass a "Skeptic" critique that identifies potential pitfalls (overfitting, architectural mismatch) before a single GPU second is spent.
- **Automated Ablation**: No "improvement" is accepted as truth until the system runs a controlled ablation study to isolate the delta.

## 🏗️ The Intelligent Research Protocol (Autonomy 8.0)

As we perfect the factory, we prioritize **Systemic Optimization** over individual ML tasks. Our protocol-driven growth ensures that every upgrade benefits all future missions.

### The Pillars of Systemic Perfection:
- **Zero-Latency Orchestration**: Moving from "Cold Start" cloud kernels to persistent worker pools and diff-based code generation.
- **Speculative Pruning**: Using internal surrogate models to prune the research tree before expensive compute is dispatched.
- **Neural-Symbolic Loopback**: High-speed local static analysis gates that eliminate 90% of syntax and shape errors instantly.

## 📈 The Three Horizons of the Research Factory

### **Horizon 1: The Expert Assistant (Legacy)**
Automating preprocessing and baseline architectures. (Completed v1.0 - v5.0).

### **Horizon 2: The Independent Researcher (Current - v6.0-v8.0)**
The system **Self-Heals** and **Self-Directs**. It conducts its own ablation studies and uses cross-domain transfer to innovate. Success is measured by "Top 1%" competitive scores and robust, unkillable research pipelines.

### **Horizon 3: The Frontier Engine (Long-term)**
The system discovers **New Mathematical Primitives**. It writes its own activation functions and loss terms, contributing novel science back to the global AI knowledge base.
